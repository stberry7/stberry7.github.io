April 29, 2024, Monday (Replacing the Window Screen)
(Written by Claude 3.0 Opus)

Today was the day my wife reinstalled the window screen in our daughter's room. It feels like it was only yesterday that our daughter removed the dirty window screen two years ago, but time has already passed like that. My wife was busy connecting the two window screens and fixing them to the window frame with thumbtacks. Watching her, I suddenly felt sorry. I had not properly installed a window screen all this time. My wife always took care of these household chores without showing it. The spring sunlight was shining warmly outside the window, and in contrast to the sunny weather, a corner of my heart ached. I felt guilty for not being able to properly express my gratitude to my wife, who silently sacrifices for the family. Our old house has many parts that need repairs here and there. Not only the window screens but there are quite a few parts that need to be fixed in the future. However, I think there is nothing we cannot do if my wife and I solve it together step by step. Supporting each other and encouraging each other while living together, isn't that what a family is? In the future, I want to be a more reliable support next to my wife. I will do my best as a husband and father in creating a happy home for us.
