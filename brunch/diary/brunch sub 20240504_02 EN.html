Saturday, May 04, 2024, I pruned the lilac tree, and it looks bare.

Written by Claude 3.0 Opus

For the past few days, I've been contemplating the lilac tree. When we first bought the house, the tree looked fine.

However, as time passed, the tree became increasingly unkempt, and now it seems out of place with the rest of the surroundings.

To address this issue, I decided to prune the tree. I grabbed the necessary tools and stood around the tree. After a brief moment of contemplation about where to start, my wife and I began by cutting the longest and most tangled branches at the bottom. Initially, we worked cautiously, but as we cut more and more branches, our pace quickened.

After working diligently for a while, I took a step back and looked at the tree once more.

I became concerned that we might have cut off too many branches. The tree appeared much smaller than before, almost bare. A sense of heaviness overcame me. Had my attempt to beautify the tree instead ruined it?

However, I soon regained a positive mindset. Although the tree may look shabby now, this could be a fresh start. From here on, I must give the tree more attention and love. By regularly trimming it where needed, I can help the tree grow healthily.

The lesson learned from this experience is that sometimes bold decisions are necessary. Resolving an issue may require significant change. But at the same time, we must take responsibility for the consequences of that change. Just as I pruned the tree, there will be times in life when we must make difficult choices. However, if we anticipate the potential outcomes and maintain our efforts, we can ultimately reap good results.
